Islamic History Department

Islamic History

The Department of Islamic History in TKM College is the earliest full-fledged department, established in the academic year 1980-81. The department attempts to teach the whole range of Islamic culture, civilizations and modern tendencies besides the religion of Islam. Department offers B.A Islamic History Degree Programme along with Indian History and Economics as subsidiary subjects, under choice based credits and semester system (CBCSS) with a maximum intake of 60 students. The department presently has two permanent faculties and guest faculties.


“Easria” – Lecture Series


Department initiated a series of lecture about the contemporary international and national issues and challenges. To inculcate social values and responsibilities among the students, department is organising programmes like Communal Harmony Campaign Week and National Integration programmes every year. It provides financial assistance and counselling to the students to resolve their economic, psychological, academic and career related issues. Department achieved best academic result in the last academic year (2019-2020) with a pass percentage of 82%.


The department comprises ten faculty members including four Ph.D. holders who have research papers published in leading international journals and books, editorial experiences, pieces of creative writing like anthologies of poems, international seminar papers to their credit. Their areas of expertise range from creative writing to comparative literature to modern literary and cultural trends to media studies. The varied interests and talents also put them in the lead when it comes to interdepartmental co-curricular activities.
