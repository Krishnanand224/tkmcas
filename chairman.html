THE CHAIRMAN

-Dr. Shahal Hassan Musaliar

TKM College of Arts and Science, the venerable citadel of learning envisioned and founded by Janab. Thangal Kunju Musaliar, the cashew king of Kerala, Is in the 6th glorious decade of its academic journey. We have always believed that education has a crucial role to play in reforming society by inculcating values into the minds of the younger generation, alongside academics. The last 57 years of the institution has witnessed the efflorescence of academic exuberance and achievements that stand the test of the time.


It is gratifying that the institution has succeeded in augmenting the human resource reservoir of the nation in a spectacular manner and that the alumni of the college adorn innumerable illustrious positions across the globe.


I exhort all the members of the Arts and Science college family to rededicate themselves to their academic endeavours.
