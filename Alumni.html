Alumni

Alumni Association of the College started function in 1990 under the able-chairmanship of Sri. Siyavudeen Musaliar. Alumni Association creates and maintains a life-long connection with its alma matter. The Alumni Association works to connect alumni, support students and build a memorable institute experience through a diversity of events and programms. The Alumni association was officially inaugurated on 26th January, 1990, by the District and Session Judge of Kollam, Sri. Sudhakaran.

In the beginning, college alumni had given scholarships and cash awards to the ward of former students, outstanding performers and students from economically weaker families. The students were selected on merit cum means basis. The Alumni association is organizing:


-Annual get together


-Honoring former teachers, principals and members of non-teaching staff


-Honoring rank holders, and former students who have outstanding performance in the various walks of life


-Honoring top performers of the children of Alumni members studying in TKMCAS who excelled at the higher secondary and graduate level


-Honoring PhD. Awardees


-Conducting send off function to retiring staff members
