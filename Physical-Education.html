Physical Education Department

Physical Education

The College has a dynamic, active and vibrant Physical Education Department. The department has state-of-art facilities for health status evaluation, health & physical education, sports & games practice, fitness development and first aid. It also has a well-equipped gymnasium in the main campus with multi-purpose exercise machines for the students and staff community of the college who aspire for physical well-being and competitive sport. The department provides opportunities for the students to demonstrate their competence by organizing intra-college competitions in various sports and games. The department conducts selection trails and camps to select college sports teams to participate in the inter-collegiate tournaments. It gives administrative support and coordinates the activities of the College Sports Club, which is committed to provide a healthy sporting habit among the students and faculties by organizing competitions, workshops, seminars and lectures on special occasions. Additionally, the department offers an open course titled Health and Fitness Education.
